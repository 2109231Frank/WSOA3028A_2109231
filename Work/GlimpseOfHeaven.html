A GLIMPSE OF HEAVEN



The client who this artwork was made for contacted me and we had a consultation. She wanted something for her mother's new home. She explained that she had a massive flatscreen that they use as an art frame one their wall, and thus I painted her the piece and it is now displayed in HD quality in their home. She told me that her mum loved looking at the clouds and that sunrays remind her of her husband (who passed), I thus tried to create something that the client's mother could look at everyday and feel calm and happy.
