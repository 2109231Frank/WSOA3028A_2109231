Blog 1 - About Me

2020-02-05

Hi, my name is Mike Oxlung.
I am 30 years old as of last week, I have a degree in abstract art and on in poetry.
This degree was extremely cool for me to study, but the only part of it that actually
helped me find a job was the design aspect of it seeing that I ended up going into
marketing. I work for a company that shall remain unnamed in case I say some fucked
up stuff that they don't like. I don't exactly LOVE my job, but it does rake in a
decent amount of money so it's worth the struggles.

I am the type of man that gets frustrated easily so this website of mine will definitely
be filled with rants and criticisms. I decided to make this website because the things
that I get passionate about tend to send me down rabbit-holes that annoy my companions
when I start talking. Feel free to interact with me (these services will be added soon)
and feel free to never come back here again - frankly, I don't give a crap either way.

The things that interest me are:

  • Art.
  • Poetry.
  • Video Games.
  • Music.
  • Combat Sports.
  • Bikes and Cars.
The that I hate are:
  • Politics.
  • The News.
  • Hipsters.
  • Assholes.
  • Cricket.

So yeah, this is the end of my first blog. I am planning to treat this website
like a diary, so I shall add another entry when I feel like talking about something,
but for now, goodbye.
